Expectations
All Ninjas must adhere to the following expectations.
Required Personal Items
Ninjas must bring the items below to every event they sign up for:
Laptop with WiFi capabilities
Headphones
Pencil or pen
Email address (must memorize the password)
We are lenient with laptops, since not all families can afford their own. The library allows us to loan out a few devices, but try not to abuse this privelage.
The email address is necessary because we use coding websites which require email logins.
Attendance
Signing up for an event means you sign up for the entire event.
Ninjas are expected to attend all dates. Failure to do so without a reasonable explanation may result in us unfortunately blacklisting the child.
We enforce this because our events are first-come, first-served, which means not attending the event steals the experience from other Ninjas.
Dojo Equipment
Please be mindful of all equipment we use in events, as they are expensive and come from our limited budget.
Cleanup
Please be courteous and clean up after yourself.
Conduct Towards Others
Harassment, intimidation, bullying, and other malicious acts are prohibited.
This includes cyberbullying.
Treat your peers and the volunteer mentors with respect.

Who Should Join
We host events for children in grades four-five, and six-eight. Unless we are running a one-day event, we tend to separate the groups, as it allows us to focus on making everyone in an event feel comfortable.
Frequently Asked Questions
Can my child join if they have never programmed before?
Children without prior coding experience can still join our Dojo! We welcome newbies and pros alike!
What should I do if my child is uninterested in your program?
We try to make the experience fun for everyone, so there is no harm in your child trying our program out.
How much does it cost to sign my child up?
Nothing! Our events are completely free!
To sign your child up, please go to How to Sign Up.
